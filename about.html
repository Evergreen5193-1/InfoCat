About InfoCat

From a Governor, For Governors

Ever felt like you were drowning in data? Spending more time in spreadsheets than in the the game? As a long-time Rise of Kingdoms player, I did. I loved the strategy, the community, and the thrill of KvK, but I knew there had to be a better way to track my progress without the manual grind.

That's why InfoCat was born.

InfoCat started as a personal project to solve a simple problem: how to make data tracking on my account easy, automated, and actually useful. The goal was to build a tool that could take the raw numbers from the game and turn them into clear, visual insights that help you make smarter decisions.

Our mission is simple: Track progress. Simplify Data. Optimize gameplay.

The Creator

InfoCat is a passion project created and maintained by me, Evergreen. I'm just another governor who loves this game and enjoys building tools that help the community. Every feature, from the trackers to the calculators, was designed to answer a question I had myself.

Join the Community

InfoCat is more than just a tool; it's a community project. The future of this platform will be shaped by your feedback and suggestions. If you have an idea, find a bug, or just want to talk RoK strategy, the best place to do it is our Discord server.
